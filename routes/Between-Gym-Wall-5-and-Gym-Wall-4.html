Between Gym Wall 5 and Gym Wall 4

First Ascent

natiboigaming

First Redpoint

natiboigaming

Buffs Used

Description

Keep your hands in front of you or on the side walls near the corners. Keep your feet as high as possible, and your arms bent. Don't rush, move slowly, but FEEL FREE to stop and shake out to regain stamina, the route is very taxing. The following changes are all recommended but not required: - Disable Survival mode - Mod for unlimited chalk (chalk won't deplete) If not, feel free to reload the save when your needs deplete.
