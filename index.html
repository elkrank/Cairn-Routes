Welcome to Cairn Routes

This is a collection of routes that the community of Cairn has accomplished and want to share with all climbers of the mountain of Kami (and hopefully others in the future).

  • "Home" takes you back here at anytime.
  • "Routes" will take you to a list of all available routes.
  • "About" takes you to a contributer page.
  • "Donate" takes you to buymeacoffee.com/pluxate to support the domain cost (primarly) and my time into this project (secondarily). I am not associated with The Game Bakers in anyway and all donations go directly to me, Pluxate.

Below is an example route page. If you have any questions, join The Game Bakers' Discord and someone should be able to answer. Also check out the Cairn wiki for game information.
